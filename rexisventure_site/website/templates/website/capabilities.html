Capability Statement

Rexis Venture Inc

Rexis Venture Inc is a small business prime supplier specializing in the sourcing and delivery of safety, industrial and laboratory products for government and commercial clients. We combine a focused supplier network with disciplined contract and vendor management.

Core competencies

  • Safety & PPE supply: head, eye, hand, hearing and respiratory protection, spill control, first aid and facility safety items.
  • Industrial & MRO products: tools, fasteners, abrasives, material handling, storage solutions, janitorial and sanitation supplies.
  • Laboratory supplies: glassware, plastics, filtration, basic reagents and general-purpose lab consumables and accessories.
  • Prime supplier & vendor management: consolidated sourcing, purchase order management, backorder coordination and substitution management.
  • Contract & compliance support: documentation, reporting and coordination aligned with government procurement requirements.

Differentiators

  • Niche-focused supplier network: we concentrate on safety, industrial and lab categories, allowing us to align products tightly with your operating environment.
  • Single point of accountability: Rexis Venture serves as the prime point of contact for sourcing, order status, issue resolution and performance reporting.
  • Agile small business: lean structure enables quick response to urgent requirements, spot buys and evolving project needs.
  • Structured processes: standardized order management, escalation paths and communication cadence with both customers and supplier partners.
  • Relationship-driven approach: we work to understand your facility, end users and operating constraints, not just the SKU list.
